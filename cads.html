CHARMINAR

Charminar is one of the oldest monuments in India, and it is situated in Hyderabad, Telangana.

BIRLA MANDIR

Birla Mandir is located towards the southern end of Hussain Sagar Lake in Hyderabad.

GOLCONDA FORT

Golconda was the principal capital of the Qutub Shahi kings. The inner fort contains ruins of palaces.
